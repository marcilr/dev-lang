Glossary for Languages

ECMAScript is the scripting language standardized by Ecma International in the ECMA-262
  specification and ISO/IEC 16262. The language is widely used for client-side scripting on
  the web, in the form of several well-known dialects such as JavaScript, JScript, and
  ActionScript.
