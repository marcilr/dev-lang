Simple Network Management Protocol (SNMP) is an Internet-standard protocol 
or collecting and organizing information about managed devices on IP networks
and for modifying that information to change device behavior. Devices that
typically support SNMP include cable modems, routers, switches, servers,
workstations, printers, and more.[1]
--wikipedia.org
