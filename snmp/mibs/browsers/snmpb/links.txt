snmpb
SnmpB is an SNMP MIB browser written in QT.  It supports SNMPv1, SNMPv2c &
SNMPv3.  SnmpB can browse/edit/load/add MIB files and can query SNMP agents.
It also supports agent discovery, trap events, and graph plotting.
sourceforge.net/projects/snmpb/
